Our Mission: Zero Waste, Zero Hunger.

We are a bridge between surplus food and community need, powered by technology and dedicated volunteers.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Protecting the environment by reducing landfill waste and CO2 emissions.

Community

Building strong local networks of donors, volunteers, and receivers.

Efficiency

Using real-time technology to ensure fast and timely food rescue.

Our Track Record

Since launching, FoodSaver has facilitated the rescue of thousands of kilograms of food, preventing massive environmental impact and supporting hundreds of community organizations. We verify quality through a mandatory video system to ensure trust and safety for all our receivers.
